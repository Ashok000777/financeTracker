Help Center

Your guide to using the Daily Expenses Application.

Overview

The Daily Expenses Application helps you track your spending, categorize expenses, and visualize your financial habits over time.

Features

  • Add Expenses: Quickly input your daily expenses.
  • View Reports: Generate reports to analyze your spending.
  • Set Budgets: Define budgets for different categories to manage your finances effectively.
  • Export Data: Export your expenses to CSV for further analysis.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, please refer to the following solutions:

  • Cannot Add Expense: Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly.
  • Report Not Generating: Check your internet connection and try again.
  • Data Not Saving: Make sure your device has sufficient storage space.
